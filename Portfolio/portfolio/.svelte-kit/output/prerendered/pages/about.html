About Me

I'm a student currently studying Interactive Digital Art and Design.

I'm passionate about animation and concept art—I love creating characters, worlds, and stories that feel alive. Art has always been my way of exploring ideas and expressing emotions.

I enjoy making games for fun, experimenting with visuals and mechanics to bring ideas to life.

I also design logos on the side, finding joy in creating simple yet impactful designs.

Recently, I've been diving into UI/UX design, where creativity meets functionality. Outside of digital art, I run a small Etsy shop where I create custom bead patterns.

I'm always learning, experimenting, and pushing myself to improve. This portfolio is a glimpse into my journey—thanks for stopping by!
